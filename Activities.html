Activities and Interests

Politics

I am very interested in politics and would definitely enjoy a career in public service. I think coming into politics with a degree in applied sciences would bring a fresh perspective to the table. Too many politicians only have a law background, and I think our government needs to be more intellectually diverse.

College Republicans.

In order to meet people with a similar interest in politics, I joined the College Republicans. We discuss current issues and occasionally assist local Republicans in their quest to get in office.

Debate Society

Debate society has been very helpful in improving my public speaking skills and debating ability. So far, I debated the merits of the death penalty and the second amendment. Debate Society is especially helpful because people give you constructive feedback after you debate.

Public Speaking

Although I get as nervous as the next person, I like talking in front of people. I participated in debate in High School, and am now in debate society. I like to argue and persuade, and pride myself on my ability to do so.

Computer Science

I love computer science, otherwise I wouldn't be majoring in it. I participated in UB's hackathon this semester, which was very exciting because it really opened my eyes to what a Computer Scientist was capable of in even a short period of time. I attended a few of UB's ACM meetings, but I had a conflict. I plan to be more active in ACM next sememster. In the meantime, I look forward to learning more about coding.
